JROTC Cybersecurity Cadets Visit

On October 27th, 2023, MistLab visits Liberty High School to give a presentation for its recent research on sensing and security to the high school students. Ruxin, Jingwei, Zeyu, and Long participate in this activity.

The topics mainly include: AR/VR, Motion-controlled Robotics, and Human Hand Recognition.

Long is giving a brief introduction of MistLab and the ongoing projects.

Ruxin is introducing her research on verifying the QR code scanner using the handgrip image, where liveness detection is perform to ensure the validity of the authencation process.

Jingwei is instructing the students on how to control a remote robotic arm using the hand movements captured by the VR device.

Zeyu talks about his project related to Cyber-physical Security, which leverages the human controller's behavioral biometrics inherited by the robotic arm to do authentication.

Students come out with many interesting quesitons during the presentation, triggering new research ideas for the lab to work on in the future.
