Liberty High School Visit Fall 2023

On October 27th, 2023, MistLab visits Liberty High School to give a presentation for its recent research on sensing and security to the high school students. Ruxin, Jingwei, Zeyu, and Long participate in this activity.

The topics mainly include: AR/VR, Motion-based User Authentication, Robotics, and Human Hand Recognition.

Long gives a brief introduction of MistLab and the ongoing projects.

Ruxin is introducing her research on verifying the QR code scanner using the handgrip image, where liveness detection is performed to ensure the validity of the authencation process.

Jingwei is instructing the students on how to control a remote robotic arm using the hand movements captured by the VR device.

Zeyu introduces his projects related to motion-based user authentication, which includes motion capture and motion analysis.

Students express a big interest in our research and experience the devices used for our projects.
