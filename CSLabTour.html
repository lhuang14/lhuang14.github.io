LSU CS Lab Tour

On Februrary 9th, 2024, MistLab hosts CS lab tours for LSU's prospective students and their parents and give a presentation about its current research. Ruxin, Jingwei, Zeyu, and Long participate in this activity.

The topics mainly include: Eating Behavior Monitoring, Behavioral Biometric Authentication, and Acoustic Sensing.

Ruxin is introducing her research on eating behavior monitoring, which uses the sensors embedded in AR device to detect chewing and swallowing.

Jingwei introduces his project that uses laser vibrometer to reconstruct the surface vibration, which can be used for speech eavesdropping.

Zeyu is talking about his research on behavioral biometrics, which uses people's hand motion for user authentication.

Long is introducing his projects related to acoustic sensing, including pedestrain identification and gripping hand verification.

Students and parents listen to the talk and ask questions.
