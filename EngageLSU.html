ENGage LSU

On March 13th, 2024, MistLab gives a presentation to the participants of ENGage LSU, who are high school students from the Baton Rouge area. Ruxin, Jingwei, Zeyu, and Long participate in this activity.

The topics mainly include: Eating/Drinking Behavior Monitroing, Robotics, and Acoustic Sensing.

Ruxin talks about her projects on eating/drinking behavior monitoring leveraging the sensors on the AR device.

Zeyu in introducing his research related to robotics and behavioral biometric authentication.

Long introduces his work on acoustic sensing, including pedestrain identification and gripping hand verification.

Students closely exprience the devices used for our projects and ask a few interesting questions.
