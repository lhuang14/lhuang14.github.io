LSU's Eye of the Tiger Event

On Februrary 23rd, 2024, MistLab gives a presentation to the participants of LSU's Eye of the Tiger Event. Ruxin, Jingwei, Zeyu, and Long participate in this activity.

The topics mainly include: Behavioral Biometric Authentication, AR/VR and Robotics.

Ruxin is introducing her research on eating behavior monitoring, which uses the sensors embedded in AR device to detect chewing and swallowing.

Jingwei introduces his audio eavesdropping project where he uses laser vibrometer to reconstruct the surface vibration induced by sound.

Zeyu talks about his research on robotics, where he implements controlling the robotic arm using the VR device.

Students and their parents show big interests in our research.
