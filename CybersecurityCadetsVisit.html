JROTC Cybersecurity Cadets Visit

On September 22nd, 2023, MistLab hosts the JROTC Cybersecurity Cadets Visit and gives a presentaiton to the students from Liberty High School for its research on cyber security. Dr.Wang, Ruxin, Zeyu, Jingwei, and Long participate in this activity.

The topics mainly include: Distracted Driving Prevention, AR/VR, Cyber-physcial Security, and Mobile Sensing.

Ruxin is introducing her research on Distracted Driving Prevention, AR/VR, and mobile sensing.

Zeyu talks about his projects related to Cyber-physical security, which leverage the human controller's behavioral biometrics inherited by the robotic arm to do authentication.

Lab members introduce their projects.

Students experience the devices/platforms.
