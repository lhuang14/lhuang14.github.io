JROTC Cybersecurity Cadets Visit

On September 22nd, 2023, MistLab hosts the JROTC Cybersecurity Cadets Visit and gives a presentaiton to the students from Liberty High School for its research on cyber security. Dr.Wang, Ruxin, Zeyu, Jingwei, and Long participate in this activity.

The topics mainly include: Distracted Driving Prevention, AR/VR, Cyber-physcial Security, and Mobile Sensing.

Ruxin is introducing her research on Preventing Distracted Driving , where she develops a smartphone application to continuously detect whether the smartphone is held in the driver's hand.

Zeyu talks about his project related to Cyber-physical Security, which leverages the human controller's behavioral biometrics inherited by the robotic arm to do authentication.

Students come out with many interesting quesitons during the presentation, triggering new research ideas for the lab to work on in the future.
